About the Museum

Established in 1950, our museum is a beacon of cultural heritage, housing a diverse collection of art from various epochs and regions.

We are committed to preserving artistic legacies and making them accessible to everyone. Through curated exhibitions and educational programs, we connect the past, present, and future of art.
